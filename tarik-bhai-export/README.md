# Tarik Bhai AI 🤍

A mobile-first, highly interactive AI website that feels like a real caring Indian brother helping users emotionally and in dangerous situations.

## Features
- **Emotional Chat:** AI responds in Hinglish with a caring, protective tone.
- **Voice AI:** Tap to speak, and the AI replies with voice.
- **Emergency Panic Button:** Grabs live GPS location and redirects to WhatsApp with an SOS message.
- **Safe Mode UI:** Quick options for immediate emotional support.
- **Helplines:** Direct links to Indian emergency numbers (112, 1091).

## How to Deploy on Netlify (3 Simple Steps)

1. **Add Your API Key:**
   Open `script.js` and replace `YOUR_API_KEY` on line 1 with your actual Google Gemini API Key.
   *(Get your free key from: https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey)*

2. **Upload to Netlify:**
   Go to [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop) and simply drag and drop this entire unzipped folder into the browser.

3. **Done!**
   Netlify will instantly give you a live URL. Your app is now live and ready to use!
